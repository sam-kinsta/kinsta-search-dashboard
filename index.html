Interpretation Notes

  • Revenue (+5.8%) and Purchases (+7.7%) both growing despite traffic decline — conversion rate and average order value improving.
  • Large bandwidth-plan revenue increases partly reflect Kinsta's migration from visits-based to bandwidth-based billing (see /blog/bandwidth-based-plans/). Prior-period "Visits" variants and current-period "Bandwidth" variants are the same plan tier.
  • Jun 22 session spike caused by new bot-traffic content pages — not a ranking event. GSC clicks for that date are normal.
